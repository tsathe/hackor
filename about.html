What's this all about?

Hack the OR 2018 is a one-day action packed event in which students, residents, attendings and individuals from many disciplines will come together to solve problems to improve the surgical experience for both patients and providers.

What can I expect?

Our event will replicate the healthcare hackathon model successfully tried at both MIT and Yale. Briefly, pain points in the surgical process or operating room will be presented in the morning. Solutions will be pitched and teams will be formed to further develop them. Chalk-talk style presentations will be given in the evening in front of a panel of judges for the chance to win cash prizes!
